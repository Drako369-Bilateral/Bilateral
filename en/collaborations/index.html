Work with me

One principle, many ways of bringing it into your work.

Whether you want to work on your own gesture or bring Bilateral Education where you train, treat or teach, the starting point is the same: which side movement is born from. Only the format changes.

The formats

The first two have a price because they’re standard. The others don’t: they’re built on your context, and the quote comes after understanding what’s needed.

Before deciding, watch me work

A method is hard to judge on paper. Before talking about quotes and calendars, the sensible thing is to watch it happen — and there are two ways, both free.

Come to a walk

Every Tuesday at 7pm I run an open meeting along the Naviglio Martesana: forty-five minutes, free. Come as a participant and watch how I work with a group I don’t know — the most honest way to assess someone. How it works →

Host one in your space

I bring the same introductory session to your members, students or patients: a single meeting, at no cost and with no commitment to continue. You see how the people you already know respond, I get to understand your context.

How we get to the right format

Step zero

A trial, at no cost

Come to a Tuesday walk, or have me run one at your place: a single meeting, free and with no commitment to continue.

First step

You tell me the context

What you do, who you work with, what’s missing. I reply within twenty-four hours and, if it doesn’t make sense, I’ll say so.

Second step

We build the right format

A session, a series, a workshop, training for your coaches: the format comes from your needs and your calendar, not from a catalogue.

Tell me what you need

Two lines are enough: who you are, what you do and what you’d like to change. If the right format isn’t any of the above, I’ll tell you — and if I’m not the right person, I’ll tell you that too.

Got it. I’ll get back to you within twenty-four hours.
