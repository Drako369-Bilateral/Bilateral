It works for the whole centre, not one discipline

The weak side exists in every sport: the weak foot in football, the weak hand in basketball, the single stance in combat sports, the same rotation direction in gymnastics. That’s why the module isn’t tied to one discipline: it’s a layer beneath them all.

Junior programmes · Athletics · Basketball · Football · Volleyball · Tennis · Artistic gymnastics · Adult gymnastics

With the coach, not instead of them

I replace nobody and I don’t touch technique: that stays the job of whoever coaches. I train the coach and work alongside them, so the competence stays in the centre even when the module ends.

The method comes from research built to be tested, with observable markers and falsifiable hypotheses. It’s movement education, not therapy, and it stays within that boundary. The research, with the documents →
